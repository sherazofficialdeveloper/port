About BISP & Unauthorized Agents

Banazir Income Support Program (BISP) is a government initiative aimed at providing financial assistance to deserving families. Unfortunately, some unauthorized agents pretend to help beneficiaries but instead misuse or steal the funds.

It is important to be aware and cautious. Always verify agents through official channels and never share sensitive information.

Safety Tips

  • Always verify agents through official BISP channels.
  • Never share your CNIC or bank details with unauthorized people.
  • Use official BISP helpline numbers for inquiries.
  • Report any suspicious agent immediately through this portal.
  • Keep your personal documents safe and confidential.
